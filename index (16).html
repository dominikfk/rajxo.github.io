Rajxo VR is at the forefront of virtual reality innovation. Our commitment to high-quality manufacturing ensures that our VR systems deliver stunning visual clarity and true-to-life experiences.

Let's dive into the technical magic:

  • 6DoF Tracking (Six Degrees of Freedom): Our VR headsets offer complete freedom of movement. 6DoF tracks both rotational and translational movements, allowing you to lean, duck, and even walk around in VR.
  • Inside-Out Tracking: Our onboard cameras use sophisticated machine vision software to determine your position in 3D space. No external markers needed! You can even create virtual barriers to prevent accidental collisions.
  • Benefits of 6DoF and Inside-Out Tracking: Enhanced immersion, greater interactivity, improved spatial awareness, and a wider range of applications.
